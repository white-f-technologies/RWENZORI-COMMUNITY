About Us

Welcome to the Rwenzori Community Conservation and Rehabilitation (RCCR), a dedicated organization committed to empowering local communities and preserving the natural environment. Our mission is to ensure a sustainable future for both the people and the unique biodiversity of the Rwenzori region, located at the heart of East Africa.

Our Vision

To build resilient communities that thrive alongside nature, where conservation and sustainable development go hand in hand, benefiting both the environment and the people who depend on it.

Our Mission

We are committed to the sustainable conservation and rehabilitation of the Rwenzori Mountains and its surrounding ecosystems by promoting community involvement in environmental protection, climate change mitigation, child welfare, and eco-friendly tourism.

Our Core Programs

Community Tourism

We offer unique cultural experiences, guided treks, and eco-tours, allowing visitors to connect with nature while contributing to the livelihood of local communities through responsible tourism.

Child Protection

We provide educational programs, vocational training, and awareness campaigns to protect children from abuse, neglect, and exploitation, building safe environments for them to thrive.

Climate Change Solutions

We implement sustainable practices like reforestation and renewable energy promotion, engaging communities to adapt to climate challenges and ensure a resilient future.

Recreation Activities

We organize activities like hiking, bird watching, and sports to foster social bonding, promote health, and encourage environmental stewardship among residents.

Environmental Rehabilitation

Our projects restore natural habitats, rehabilitate degraded land, and conserve water resources through education and community collaboration.

Why Choose Us?

  • Community-Driven Approach: We prioritize local involvement, ensuring communities benefit directly and have ownership of our efforts.
  • Sustainable Practices: Our programs are designed for long-term environmental and community benefits.
  • Holistic Development: We address interconnected challenges of climate, child protection, and sustainable development.
  • Partnerships: We collaborate with governments, NGOs, and private sectors for maximum impact.
