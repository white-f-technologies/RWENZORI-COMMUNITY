CHILD PROTECTION INITIATIVES

At RCCR, we are deeply committed to the safety, well-being, and holistic development of every child. Our child protection initiatives are a cornerstone of our mission to create a nurturing and secure environment where children can thrive, free from harm and fear.

We believe that safeguarding children is not just a responsibility, it is a moral imperative. Our efforts are guided by international child protection standards, national laws, and a commitment to uphold the rights of every child through education, community outreach, and robust policies.

We invite you to join us in this vital mission.

Explore our website to learn more about our programs, partnerships, and how you can contribute to creating a safer world for children.

Our Programs

We work across various programs and initiatives to protect children from all forms of harm. Here are some of the key areas we focus on:


1. Child Abuse Prevention

Child abuse takes many forms—physical, emotional, sexual, and neglect. Through our Child Abuse Prevention Program, we educate parents, teachers, and community members about the signs of abuse and the resources available to protect children.


2. Support for At-Risk Children

Our At-Risk Children Support Program focuses on identifying children who are most vulnerable to abuse, neglect, or exploitation, including those in foster care, homeless children, and children in conflict zones.


3. Child Labor and Exploitation Prevention

Every child has the right to a childhood free of labor and exploitation. We are dedicated to ending child labor through education, advocacy, and partnerships with global organizations to eliminate exploitative practices.


4. Emergency Response and Disaster Relief for Children

Natural disasters, conflict, and crises often leave children especially vulnerable. Our Emergency Response Program ensures that children in disaster-stricken areas receive immediate protection, care, and access to healthcare.
